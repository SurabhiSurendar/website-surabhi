About Me

Hello, I am Surabhi, a graduate student pursuing a Master’s in Engineering Management with an analytics track at North Carolina State University. With a strong foundation in data analysis, project management and business processes, I am passionate about using data-driven insights to solve complex problems and deliver impactful results.

These experiences have equipped me to excel in roles that require bridging the gap between technical teams and business stakeholders.

Driven by the challenge of driving innovation and efficiency in products, projects, and business operations, I focus on analyzing data to uncover insights, managing cross-functional initiatives, and optimizing workflows. Let’s connect to explore how my skills can contribute to your team’s success!
